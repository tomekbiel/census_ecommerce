E-commerce Data Visualizations

1. Monthly Revenue Trend

Shows the total revenue generated each month over time, helping identify sales trends and seasonality.

2. Average Order Value Over Time

Tracks changes in the average order value, which can indicate changes in customer purchasing behavior.

3. Sales by Day of Week

Shows which days of the week generate the most sales, useful for staffing and marketing decisions.

4. Top Selling Categories

Displays the highest revenue-generating product categories.

5. Customer Retention Heatmap

Shows how well customers are retained over time, with cohorts based on their first purchase month.

6. Customer Acquisition

Tracks the number of new customers acquired each month.

7. Order Value Distribution

Shows the frequency distribution of order values, helping understand typical purchase amounts.

8. Top Products by Revenue

Lists the top 10 products by total revenue generated.

9. Customer Region Distribution

Shows where your customers are located by region.

10. Monthly Active Customers

Tracks the number of unique customers making purchases each month.

11. Order Correlation Matrix

Shows the correlation between different order metrics.

12. Order Date Distributions

Shows the distribution of orders over different time periods.

13. Order Numerical Distributions

Shows statistical distributions of numerical order data.

14. Shopify Sales Analysis

Analysis of Shopify sales data.
